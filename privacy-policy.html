Security of Private Information

MySetAside uses SSL encryption using a GoDaddy SSL Certificate security certificate (https://www.godaddy.com/web-security/ssl-certificate ) to protect sensitive information online. We take several precautions to protect user-information off-line. All of our users’ information has restricted access. Cookies

MySetAside uses session ID cookies. These are bits of text that are temporarily set on your computer. These are used to make it easier for you to navigate our Website. For example, thanks to cookies you do not have to log-in repeatedly (from Web page to Web page). A session ID cookie expires when you close your browser. We do not link the information we store in cookies to any PII you submit while on our site. Third Parties providing services for our Web site also set cookies.
